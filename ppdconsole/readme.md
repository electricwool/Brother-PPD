The PPD console such as ppd-120 or kc310 utilizes a very old TV tuner that can burn out and stops working properly. It also comes in various flavors utilizing an old analog TV tuner with cartiridges in various television formats. Cheap RF recievers did not work with mine. one was PAL and one was NTSC-J which is not supported at all by most RF recievers.

The PPD console contains a sony CXA1145P RGB Encoder. This chip is a complete solution for outputting analog video. it allows users to easily bypass the need for an 80s japanese television by bypassing the TV tuner.

simply connect a RCA pigtail or panel jack to these points in order to get RCA composite video output. ziptie part of the cable to one of the central posts to prevent the cable from pulling out.
